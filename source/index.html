Felag
"Wellington Medieval
Armoured Combat"
club

Felag ("fellowship" in Old Norse) is a Wellington based historical medieval combat fight club. We are a diverse club with a strong social aspect that reflects through our camaraderie in and out of the list (fighting arena). We offer a variety of activities beyond fighting including event organisation, managing the list, and even earning certifications that look great on a CV!

How to Join

Sign up for the intake to join our basic course.

Time

Mondays 7 – 9 p.m.
Wednesdays 7 – 9 p.m.
Thursday 7 – 9 p.m.
Optional extra Saturday training 10am –2pm (armour and steel training)

Location

Ricoh Sports Centre at Fraser Park Sportsville Inc
237 Taita Drive, Avalon, Lower Hutt 5011, New Zealand.
Plenty of onsite car parking.

Cost

Our intake training sessions are $50 per person for the 5-week course.
Contact us to know more and book your place on our next intake.
